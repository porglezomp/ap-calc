$$\newcommand{\dd}{\mathrm{d}}$$
## The Fundamental Theorum
The fundamental theorum of calculus is
$$ \frac{\dd y}{\dd x} = \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} $$
## Vector Calculus
Vector calculus is
$$
\nabla f = 
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{\dd f}{\dd x} &
\frac{\dd f}{\dd y} &
\frac{\dd f}{\dd z} &
...
\end{bmatrix}
$$
