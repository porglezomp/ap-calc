### The Fundamental Theorum
The fundamental theorum of calculus is
$$
\newcommand{\dd}{\mathrm{d}}
\frac{\dd y}{\dd x} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x}
$$
### Vector Calculus
Vector calculus is
$$
\nabla f = 
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{\dd f}{\dd x} &
\frac{\dd f}{\dd y} &
\frac{\dd f}{\dd z} &
...
\end{bmatrix}
$$
